Best Projects & Paper Publications:

Best Projects of the Department are being awarded to the students who excel well in designing and fabrication of working models of Robotics and Automation systems. The following components are considered for evaluating the best projects:

  • Innovation in projects that caters to societal needs.
  • Accurate design calculation.
  • Commissioning and demonstration of working model.
